Making of Hyderabadi Biryani

step1:I have used Fried Onions as I had some.
step2:If you fry Onions in Oil and then use that Oil in the Biryani, the Biryani will get a nice aroma.
step3:Chicken Biryani tastes better with more Ghee.
step4:If you add Lemon Juice to Rice while it is boiling, then the Rice stays white.
step5:The Rice should be added only when the Water is boiling,or Rice becomes sticky,and the grains are not
fluffy.
step6:Salt should be more in Biryani. If you taste the Water, it should taste like seawater.
step7:It is only the Salt that you add to boiling water that gets into the Biryani.
step8:The Salt does not stick even if you add later to the Biryani.
step9:So you must check Salt while the Rice is added to the boiling Water.


MAKING OF AMBUR BRIYANI

step1:Soak both the dry red chillies in hot water for 5 mins. Drain and make it into a smooth puree.
step2:Now heat oil in a kadai. Add in cinnamon, cloves, cardamom and fry for a min.
step3:Pour in 1 tblspn of curd and saute for 30 sec.
step4:Add in a handful of onions and saute till golden.
step5:Add in garlic paste and saute for 2 mins.
step6:Sprinkle in coriander leaves and mint leaves. Saute for a min.
step7:Add in ginger paste and saute for 2 mins.
step8:Now add in the chicken and toss well on high heat for 5 to 7 mins till it is golden.
step9:Add in onions and tomatoes. Saute for 3 to 4 mins. They will cook down and leach out some water.
step10:Now add in the dry red chilli paste and mix well.
step11:Add in Â½ cup of curd and salt. Mix well. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 10 mins.
step12:In the mean time, bring lots of water to boil. Add in salt to it and when it boils add in rice. Mix well. Cook it for 7 to 8 mins.
step13:When you taste it the rice should have a bite in it. Once it is 80 percent cooked, drain it.
step14:Now the chicken will be cooked.. Add rice to a large pot, add in curry and mix well.
step15:Cover with a tight lid and place the whole thing over a tawa and cook on low heat for 10 to 12 mins.
step16:Turn off the heat and leave it for 15 mins.
step17:Now open the lid and fluff the rice.
step18:Serve with raita.
